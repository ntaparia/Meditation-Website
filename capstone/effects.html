Effects of Meditation

Timeline of usual effects

WeeksEffects of meditation
1Mind Wandering barely slows down.
2Mind Wandering slows down temporarily although anxiety continues to persist.
3Start to become aware of the repetition of thoughts. Anxiety begins to reduce and focus on work improves.
4Empathy begins to improve. Because of increase of focus on the present moment, productivity greatly imporves, and anxiety decreases.
5Positive emotions towards oneself begins to increase, and negative thinking begins to decrease.
6"Me" Thoughts decrease while increase of thoughts for others.
7 Immunity and memory improves dramatically due to less stress and rewiring of brain.
8The amygdala shrinks and more activation in the left prefrontal cortex occurs. Portrays decrease in anxiety and increase in emotional health
