Why Meditation?

Meditation is a practice where an individual focuses one's attention on a thought, object, or activity to train attention and achieve an emotionally calm state. In recent times, where social media has the norm among the young population, the increase in levels of anxiety, stress, and depression. In addition, the average attention span of a normal individual has decreased to only two seconds per person which has lead to a decrease in productivity. By improving our emotional state and attention,we can conquer anxiety, depression, and attention disorders. Meditation is one of the only natural ways to attack the mental illness and productivity issue facing the western world today.
